About HackCville

HackCville develops the skills, networks, and entrepreneurial ability of talented U.Va. students. We accelerate our students’ ideas, projects, and startups through our experiential programs and tight-knit community.

In turn, we provide talent to 50+ companies in Charlottesville’s growing tech and startup scene and empower top students to start companies of their own. Our home is our clubhouses near the University of Virginia, but our broader network of 1,200+ spans the globe.
